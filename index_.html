Liu Research Lab @Brown


Welcome. This research lab is led by Dr Liu Tao and consists of a group of young statisticians, computer scientists, and applied mathematicians. Our group focus on developing novel statistical methods with application to various scientific domains. Our current works are motivated by issues that arise when attempting to statistically model massive data sets. Our research interests include (not limited to) causal inference, statistical/machine learning, longitudinal/survival data, and optimal decision making. As a group, we welcome investigators looking for interdisciplinary collaborations and students to join our team.
