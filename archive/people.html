Tao Liu Ph.D. is Associate Professor of Biostatistics at Brown University Department of Biostatistics. He is affiliated with Brown Center for Statistical Sciences (CSS), Data Science Initiative (DSI). Prior to joining Brown, he received his PhD from the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Pennsylvania, where he was advised by Dr Daniel Heitjan. Dr. Liu received his Master of Science degree in Statistics from Iowa State University and Bachelors degree from Tsinghua University.
